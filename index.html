About Me

I am a machine learning researcher with expertise in **deep learning, optimization, and signal processing**. My research focuses on **fair and invariant representation learning**, **adversarial robustness**, and **high-dimensional data processing**.

I have contributed to multiple NSF-funded research projects and have authored **high-impact journal and conference papers** in **TMLR, IEEE TSP, CVPR, ICCV, ECML-PKDD, and SampTA**. My work has also led to **multiple patents** in adversarial machine learning.

I am passionate about developing **robust ML models** and applying **mathematical optimization techniques** to improve **data fairness, privacy, and efficiency** in machine learning applications.
