About Me

I am a machine learning researcher with expertise in deep learning, optimization, and signal processing. My research focuses on developing advanced algorithms for **fair and invariant representation learning**, **adversarial robustness**, and **high-dimensional data processing**.

I have contributed to multiple NSF-funded research projects and have authored high-impact journal and conference papers in top venues such as **TMLR, IEEE TSP, CVPR, and ECML-PKDD**. My work has also led to multiple patents in adversarial machine learning.
